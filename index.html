AokiAI

For Android

aokiAI is an app designed to assist with tasks, and also allows you to find information in a simple, efficient manner. The app is equiped with a sophisticated Artificial Intelligence, allowing it to self-upgrade the UI for maximum ease of use. aokiAI is partially web-based, but does not need a constant internet connection to run. The app uses machine vision, pattern recognition, and Artificial Intelligence to predict what you want to do before you even do it. aokiAI can run applications, as it is a new platform for developing web-based applications, extensions, and more. aokiAI comes with many builtin applications: note-taking, media player, camera, reminders, and more. You can also download additional applications that will run within the aokiAI interface.
